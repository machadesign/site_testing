Game Overview

As the Word creator for your team, your goal is to use as many of the six rolled Letter blocks as possible to build a word that belongs to a chosen category. Rotate and place the Clue blocks around the letters to help your team guess the word before the time runs out. If your team fails to guess the word correctly, the other team/s have a chance to score. You have two minutes, and GO!
